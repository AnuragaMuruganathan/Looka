  • Peanut Butter Choc Chip Protein Balls

    Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins

    DESCRIPTION:

    DESCRIPTION:

    These no-bake peanut butter chocolate chip protein balls are the perfect gluten-free, dairy-free vegan treats for a quick snack or breakfast on-the-go. Only 6 ingredients required, and no need for protein powder!

    INGREDIENTS:

                       
80g rolled oats

125g
natural peanut butter

9 tbsp
ground flaxseed

3 tbsp
maple syrup

30g
sugar-free chocolate chips

1/2 tsp
vanilla extract

    STEPS TO PREPARE:

  • In a large mixing bowl, add rolled oats and ground flaxseed and mix until combined. Add peanut butter and maple syrup to a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high for 30 seconds or until peanut butter has melted. Add mixture to rolled oats along with vanilla extract.
  • Mix very well until combined. Mix in chocolate chips.
  • Using your hands, shape mixture into small balls. Enjoy right away or store in an airtight container in thefridge.
  • When shaping them into balls, you can use a measuring spoon to scoop the mixture to keep all the balls the same size.
  • To make these completely sugar-free, substitute maple syrup with a sugar-free syrup. Feel free to substitute peanut butter with any other nut or seed butter of choice.
  • I used a mixture of sugar-free dark and white chocolate chips but feel free to use whatever you have on hand instead. Other add-in’s such like chopped nuts or dried fruit would also be nice!
